American Pancakes

Description

Everyone loves American Pancakes. Fluffy, sweet and a real crowd pleaser! Serve with fruit, ice cream, bacon...whatever you like!

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 25g butter, melted plus more for frying
  • 10g caster sugar
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 200ml buttermilk

Steps

  1. Place all of the pancake ingredients into a bowl and whisk together.
  2. Fry spoonfuls of the batter in melted butter over a medium heat for one minute, before flipping over and cooking for a further minute.
  3. Serve onto plates, sprinkle over the blueberries and drizzle over the maple syrup to finish.
